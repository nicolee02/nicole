These are the projects I have made in AP Computer Science Principles.

This is my first game. I created it in Scratch. It is called Bust Down D-Money. To Play: 1. Press the flag to begin. 2. Use the mouse to control the trampoline so that the person jumps on the trampoline each time. 3. As you pass through the levels the speed of the person will increase. 4. Touch the yellow star for bonus points. 5. If you touch the lava then you die.

This is a game I created in MIT App Inventor. It is Tic Tac Toe. It vibrates every time someone wins or loses. It also plays music throughout the game and switches the type of music when someone wins.
